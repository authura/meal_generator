


BREADED PARMESAN RANCH CHICKEN

INGREDIENTS:

8 (4-6oz) boneless chicken breast halves
3/4 cup crushed corn flakes
3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 (1oz) packet ranch salad dressing mix
1/2 cup butter, melted

INSTRUCTIONS:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degreees F (175 C). Grease 9x13 baking dish.

2. Combine corn flakes, parmesan cheese, and ranch dressing mix in bowl.
Dip chicken in melted butter; roll each breast in corn flake mixture
until evenly coated. Place chicken in prepared baking dish.

3. Bake until chicken is no longer pink in center and juices run clear,
about 45 mintues.

4. Allow to cool for 5-10 minutes. Serve.

SERVES 8
