

SPAGHETTI AND MEATBALLS

INGREDIENTS:

3 tbsp olive oil
3/4 cup chopped onion
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 (16oz) cans crushed tomatoes
3 (6oz) cans tomato paste
1 cup water
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup chopped oregano
1 dried bay leaf
salt and pepper to taste
1lb ground beef
1/2 cup Italian bread crumbs
1/4 cup chopped parsley
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 (16oz) package uncooked spaghetti

INSTRUCTIONS:

1. Heat olive oil in large saucepan over medium heat, and cook the
onion until lighty brown. Mix in 2 cloves garlic and cook 1 minute.
Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water sugar, 1/2 the oregano,
and bay leaf. Season with slay and pepper. Bring to a boil, reduce
heat to low, and simmer.

2. In a bowl, mix the beef, bread crumbs, remaining oregano, remaining
garlic, parsley, eggs, and cheese. Season with salt and pepper. Roll
into 1 inch balls and drop into the sauce. Cook 40 minutes in the sauce,
or until internal temp of meatballs reaches a min of 160 degrees F.

3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil, and stir in the
spaghetti. Cook 8-10 mins until al dente, and drain. Serve the meatballs
and sauce over the cooked spaghetti.
