

TUNA CASSEROLE

INGREDIENTS:

Main Dish:
3 cups egg noodles
1 tablespoon butter
1 small onion(diced)
2 stalks celery(diced)
2/3 cup frozen peas(defrosted)
1 can tuna(5-6 oz, drained)
10 1/2 oz condensed mushroom soup
1/3 cup milk
1 cup cheddar cheese
1 tbsp parsley

Crumb Topping:
1/2 cup panko bread crumbs
1 tbsp butter
1/2 cup cheddar
1 tbsp parsley

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Combine topping ingredients and set aside.

2. Boil noodles al dente. Drain and rinse under cold water.

3. Cook onion and celery in butter until tender, 5-7 minutes.

4. In a large bowl, combine noodls, onion mixture, peas, soup, 
milk, cheese, tuna and parsely. Mix well.

5. Spread into a 2qt casserole dish and top with crumb topping.

6. Bake 18-20 minutes or until bubbly.

SERVES 4-6
