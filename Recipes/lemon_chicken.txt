

LEMON CHICKEN

INGREDIENTS:

1/4 cup olive oil
3 tbsp minced garlic (9 cloves)
1/3 cup dry white whine
1 tbsp grated lemon zest (1 or 2 lemons)
2 tbsp lemon juice
1 1/2 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp minced thyme
salt and pepper
4 boneless chicken breasts
1 lemon

INSTRUCTIONS:

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.

2. Warm olive oil in saucepan over medium-low heat, add garlic,
and cook for 1 minute w/o allowing garlic to turn brown. Off the heat,
add white whine, lemon zest, lemon juice, oregano, thyme and 1tsp salt
and pour into a 9x13 baking dish.

3. Pat breasts dry and place them over the sauce. Brush chicken breasts
with olive oil and sprinkle liberally with salt and pepper. Cut lemon into
8 wedges and tuck among the pieces of chicken.

4. Bake for 30-40 minutes, depending on the size of the chicken breasts,
until the chicken is down and top is lightly browned.

5. Allow to rest for 5-10 minutes. Sprinkle with salt, baste with juices,
and serve.

SERVES 4
